Section 3: Common Mistakes to Avoid

Over-Churning

Don't over-churn your gelato or it'll become too airy and lose that characteristic density. Stop churning once it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

Wrong Serving Temperature

Avoid serving it straight from the freezerâ€”let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes to reach the proper texture. This allows the flavors to fully develop.

Too Much Cream

Resist the urge to add too much cream; the lower fat content is what makes gelato special. Stick to the traditional milk-to-cream ratio for authentic results.
