Section 1: Key Ingredients

The secret to authentic gelato lies in using high-quality ingredients and the right proportions. Traditional gelato uses more milk than cream, which keeps the fat content lower (usually around 4-8% compared to ice cream's 14-20%).

Essential Ingredients Table

Ingredient Quantity Purpose
Whole Milk 2 cups Base liquid, lower fat content
Heavy Cream 1 cup Adds richness and smoothness
Sugar 3/4 cup Sweetness and texture
Egg Yolks 4-6 yolks Emulsification and creaminess
